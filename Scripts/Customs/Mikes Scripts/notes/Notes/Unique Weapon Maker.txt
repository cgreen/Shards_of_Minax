I would like you help making some unique weapons for my RunUO server.  Below is an example of a basic unique weapon.  The begin with it has a random hue and random damage.  It has two bonuses from the "attributes" category, bonusdex and bonusint which give bonues to their respective stats.  Next it does extra damage against Elementals in the "slayer" category.  Next in the "weaponattributes" category this sword has a 50% chance to activate HitDispel  Finally there is a bonus to Magic Resist from the "skillbonuses" category.  XmlLevelItem makes the item able to gain experiance, I want all of the unique weapons to have this.  You can use an unlimited number of attributes and weaponattributes when making the item.  You can have up to three kinds of slayer with Slayer, Slayer2, and Slayer3.  You can have up to 5 separate skill bonuses.
